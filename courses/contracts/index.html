Contract Law

When and how should law enforce voluntary agreements? This course explores the rules, cases, and problems that define contractual obligation in American law.

The Contract Law Companion includes interactive exercises — including the Bilbo's Burglar Agreement contract anatomy, a Remedies simulation, and a Battle of the Forms UCC 2-207 game — plus a statutory supplement and supplementary resources organized by chapter.
